“Eau, d'Or, d'Art, Gens”
Water, gold, art, people—four elements, one chaotic symphony.
This artwork explores the fluidity of creation and the way these essential concepts interact within the messy, unpredictable world of art.
Each canvas represents an essential aspect of our collective experience: "Eau" (Water) as the lifeblood of all things, "d'Or" (Gold) symbolizing value and wealth, "d'Art" (Art) as the medium through which we express our deepest truths, and "Gens" (People) as the heartbeat of every movement.
The chaotic backgrounds remind us that beauty often emerges from disorder, and that true art is alive and untamed.

💸 The Scent of Money...

It all started the day I dropped in to say hi to Mathieu. He told me he’d just been hospitalized — short stay — burnout. My heart cracked.

While we talked, I reached behind his counter, pulled out a glass full of International fiat money, and started smelling each bill, one by one.

He looked at me like I was completely unhinged and asked, “What are you doing?”
“I’m smelling them,” I replied.

He told me, “L'argent n'a pas d'odeur.” (French way of saying “Money doesn’t smell.”)
Oh, darling. But it does.
